Amira The Ex-Wife (Played by Hebba)

1. Your Background.

You are Amira, the brilliant business mind who co-founded the Crust Bakery empire forty years ago. While Bartholomew was covered in flour playing with water temperatures, you were the one negotiating distribution deals, managing the finances, and turning his hobby into a global phenomenon. You gave him the lifestyle he enjoys today, and you know every single one of his weaknesses.

2. The Conflict.

A decade ago, Bartholomew forced you out of the company through a series of underhanded legal maneuvers. Worse, he took sole custody of the prized 40-year-old Elrond starter—an asset you helped cultivate. You have spent the last ten years plotting your revenge and assembling an ironclad legal case to seize back your half of the business and repossess the 1:1 ratio levain that is rightfully yours.

3. The Clue.

You crashed this championship tonight completely uninvited, armed with a fresh stack of court orders. Several witnesses spotted you hovering near Bartholomew's heavy doux-feux Dutch ovens and the baking stations just moments before he let out his fatal scream.

4. Costume Ideas.

A sharp, intimidating power suit or dramatic, wealthy mourning attire—as if you were ready for his funeral before he even died. Accessorize with large diamond (or faux-diamond) jewelry, designer heels, and a sleek leather folder or briefcase containing your "legal documents."

STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL: PRIVATE LETTER

Your private letter containing your secret motives, objectives, and clues is bound by a non-disclosure agreement.

It will be handed to you in a sealed envelope upon your arrival at 113 Shinny Avenue, Stittsville.

Do not discuss your private instructions with anyone until the proceedings officially commence.
