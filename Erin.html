Your stream took a disastrous turn yesterday. You were spotted wandering near Bartholomew's station shortly after the altercation, right before he was found face-down in the flour. You claim you were just trying to salvage parts of your broken tripod, but others suspect you were looking for the perfect revenge—or at least the perfect thumbnail.

Trendy, camera-ready social media attire. Wear a stylish, vibrant outfit and accessorize with a VIP Media lanyard around your neck. Carry a smartphone attached to a visibly broken ring light, a shattered selfie stick, or a cracked tripod, and pretend to vlog the other guests' reactions throughout the night.
