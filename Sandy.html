Mei The Prodigy (Played by Sandy)

1. Your Background.

You are Mei, the youngest and most talked-about baker in the modern artisan bread world. You do not just bake; you create edible, viral art. Your social media presence is exploding, and your avant-garde techniques have completely disrupted the traditional scene. Your prized possession is your fiercely active 1:1 ratio levain, named Mithril, which is exceptionally young at less than a year old.

2. The Conflict.

Bartholomew Crust represented the dusty, boring past, and he hated you for being the future. He recently dismissed your innovative creations as "child's play" and publicly doubted the strength of your young Mithril starter, stating it lacked the depth of his 40-year-old Elrond. However, the real crisis happened last night: someone sabotaged your precious Mithril by pouring bleach directly into its jar. You immediately and loudly accused Bartholomew of trying to eliminate his biggest threat.

3. The Clue.

You were seen storming away from Bartholomew's station in tears just an hour before he was found dead. The police are looking closely at the young prodigy who had both the motive to retaliate and the modern chemistry knowledge to execute a flawless revenge.

4. Costume Ideas.

Trendy, hipster, and avant-garde baker attire. Wear colorful clothing, bold accessories, or temporary tattoos. Bring a sketchbook filled with your crazy recipe ideas, and perhaps carry a small, sad-looking empty glass jar labeled "Mithril - RIP" to garner sympathy from the other guests throughout the evening.
