Mathieu The Apprentice (Played by Dany)

1. Your Background.

You are Mathieu, Bartholomew's chronically overworked and entirely unappreciated apprentice. For years, you have been the hidden muscle behind the Crust Bakery empire, doing all the heavy lifting while Bartholomew smiled for the cameras. You know every intimate detail of the bakery's operations, but you receive absolutely none of the credit or the profits.

2. The Conflict.

Bartholomew's exploitation reached a breaking point this week. He explicitly banned you from using the Cuisinart stand mixer, forcing you to mix hundreds of pounds of stiff dough completely by hand. Worse, he recently stole your innovative recipe for dinner rolls baked in the Cuisinart steam convection oven plus, presenting it to the judges as his own masterpiece. You are tired of being treated like cheap manual labor while he steals your culinary genius.

3. The Clue.

You were the last person seen at the preparation station before Bartholomew's demise. Your specific task was to fill the sink with exactly 15C warm water to balance the 21.5C kitchen and 20C flour for his signature bulk fermentation. The police suspect the water was tampered with before Bartholomew face-planted into the flour sack.

4. Costume Ideas.

Utilitarian and exhausted. Wear a plain t-shirt and a highly functional, flour-stained apron. Smudge a little extra flour on your face and arms to show you have been doing the manual labor. Carry a heavy whisk or a dough scraper, and practice looking deeply resentful of anyone giving orders.
