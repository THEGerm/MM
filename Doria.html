Surveying the harvest...

Djamila The Farmer (Played by Doria)

1. Your Background.

You are Djamila, a proud, hardworking agriculturist who cultivates a thriving organic farm in the Ottawa Valley. You specialize in resilient heritage grains and stone-milled flours, blending the rich agricultural traditions of your Algerian roots with the rugged Canadian soil. You put your blood, sweat, and tears into the earth to produce the finest, most sought-after baking ingredients in the region.

2. The Conflict.

Bartholomew Crust has been using your premium flour for years to win his championships. However, when his recent test bakes using his 1:1 Elrond starter started failing miserably—undoubtedly due to his own negligence and rushed fermentation times—he refused to take the blame. Instead, he publicly scapegoated your harvest. He threatened to ruin your farm's reputation in the press, claiming your grains were "inferior, weak, and utterly unpredictable." You cannot let him destroy your family's livelihood to protect his own ego.

3. The Clue.

You are fiercely protective of your crops and your honor. You were seen having a screaming match with Bartholomew near the loading dock earlier today. Later, several competitors noticed you pacing angrily near his fermentation sink right before the fatal incident. The police are starting to wonder if the farmer decided to literally bury the baker in his own lies (and flour).

4. Costume Ideas.

Earthy, practical, and rugged farmer attire. Wear a plaid flannel shirt, denim overalls or durable jeans, and work boots. Accessorize with a straw hat or a bandana, and perhaps bring a small burlap sack or a handful of dried wheat stalks to proudly represent your prized heritage crop throughout the evening.

HARVEST ALERT: PRIVATE LETTER

Your private letter containing your secret motives, objectives, and clues is deeply buried.

It will be handed to you in a sealed envelope upon your arrival at 113 Shinny Avenue, Stittsville.

Do not unearth your private instructions with anyone until the dinner bell rings.
