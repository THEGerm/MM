Vikram The Corporate Executive (Played by Shazz)

1. Your Background.

You are Vikram, the ruthless VP of Acquisitions for MegaBread Corp, the world's largest and most profitable commercial bakery conglomerate. You do not care about heritage grains, artisanal crusts, or the romance of baking. You care about scale, efficiency, and market domination. Your board of directors has given you one mandate: acquire the legendary 1:1 ratio Elrond starter so MegaBread can chemically reverse-engineer it for mass production.

2. The Conflict.

Yesterday afternoon, you cornered Bartholomew Crust in the lobby and offered him a staggering multi-million dollar buyout for his bakery and the exclusive rights to the Elrond starter. Instead of taking the money, he laughed in your face. He loudly called your company a purveyor of factory sludge and tore up your contract in front of the entire international baking press. Your board is furious, and your prestigious job is now hanging by a thread.

3. The Clue.

You were seen hovering near the competitor workstations just moments before Bartholomew collapsed into the flour. You claim you were merely pacing while taking an urgent phone call from your shareholders, but several witnesses swear they saw you eyeing the glass fermentation jars with extreme desperation.

4. Costume Ideas.

An impeccably tailored, expensive-looking suit. Wear a flashy, oversized watch, a crisp pocket square, and slick back your hair if possible. Carry a sleek leather portfolio, a Montblanc pen, or a stack of aggressive buyout contracts to wave in the faces of the artisanal bakers throughout the evening.

NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT: PRIVATE LETTER

Your private letter containing your secret corporate motives, objectives, and clues is heavily classified.

It will be handed to you in a sealed envelope upon your arrival at 113 Shinny Avenue, Stittsville.

Do not leak your private instructions to the press or competitors until the negotiations officially open.
