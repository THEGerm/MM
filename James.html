Silas The Supplier (Played by James)

1. Your Background.

You are Silas, the shadowy underground broker of the baking world. While others purchase their ingredients from wholesome farms or commercial distributors, you operate out of the back of an unmarked van. You provide the exclusive, highly regulated 20C flour to the competitors. Your past is a mystery, your prices are exorbitant, and your business practices are highly illegal.

2. The Conflict.

Bartholomew Crust was your most lucrative client, but he recently became suspicious of your shipments. Earlier today, you were seen in a heated, aggressive argument with him in the alleyway behind the venue. He had run an analysis and discovered your latest batch of premium flour was actually cut with cheap, low-protein fillers. He threatened to expose your fraud to the international baking commission, a move that would permanently destroy your underground enterprise.

3. The Clue.

You were spotted slipping through the kitchen's back door just minutes before Bartholomew was found dead. Even more suspicious, the victim was discovered face-down inside a heavy sack of flour bearing your counterfeit supply labels.

4. Costume Ideas.

Shady, covert, and suspicious. Wear a sleek suit layered under a long, dark trench coat. Keep a pair of dark sunglasses on, even indoors. Carry a mysterious metal briefcase or clutch a suspicious, unmarked brown paper bag labeled "Specialty Flour" to conduct your back-alley deals throughout the evening.
