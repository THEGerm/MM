Béatrice The Bitter Rival (Played by Janick)

1. Your Background.

You are Béatrice, a strict traditionalist and a fierce defender of old-world baking techniques. To you, true artisanal bread requires sweat, discipline, and patience. Your absolute pride and joy is your 30-year-old stiff levain, named Moria, which you meticulously maintain at a highly demanding 1:2 water-to-flour ratio. You view anything else as a lazy shortcut, little better than store-bought commercial yeast.

2. The Conflict.

For years, Bartholomew Crust has been the thorn in your side. He popularized the "easy" 1:1 liquid starter—his famous Elrond—and aggressively marketed his modern, warm-water sink-fermentation method. In recent trade magazines, he even referred to your beloved stiff levain techniques as "archaic and unnecessarily difficult." You entered this championship with one goal: to humiliate him on the grand stage and prove that Moria's traditional crust and crumb are vastly superior to his watered-down methods.

3. The Clue.

Bartholomew’s entire process hinges on a perfectly balanced temperature: a 21.5C ambient kitchen environment mixed with exactly 15C water and 20C flour. During the competition, you were caught suspiciously lurking near the kitchen's main thermostat, right before the ambient room temperature plummeted. With Bartholomew now dead and his fermentation ruined, everyone is pointing fingers at the traditionalist who despised his modern environment.

4. Costume Ideas.

Traditional European baker attire. Wear a sturdy, flour-dusted canvas or linen apron over comfortable clothes. Consider pulling your hair back into a practical, no-nonsense bun. Carry a wooden mixing spoon and perhaps a small glass jar or crock boldly labeled "Moria - 1:2" to show off your superior starter.

STRICTLY CONFIDENTIAL: PRIVATE LETTER

Your private letter containing your secret motives, objectives, and clues is strictly confidential.

It will be handed to you in a sealed envelope upon your arrival at 113 Shinny Avenue, Stittsville.

Do not discuss your private instructions with anyone until the ovens are hot and the game officially begins.
