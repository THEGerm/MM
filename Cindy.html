Madam Lin The Critic (Played by Cindy)

1. Your Background.

You are Madam Lin, the undisputed sovereign of the culinary review world. Your syndicated column, "The Crumb," is read by millions. A mere sigh from you can shutter a bakery; a nod of approval guarantees a lifetime of success. You are accustomed to being treated like royalty, with executive chefs trembling as you take your first bite of their creations.

2. The Conflict.

Bartholomew Crust is the only baker who has ever dared to defy you. At the opening gala of this very championship, he served you a dense, horribly under-proofed loaf. When you gave him a harsh critique, he laughed in your face and loudly announced to the room that your palate was "as sophisticated as a wet sponge." The public humiliation was absolute. You swore he would pay for his insolence and lack of respect for your authority.

3. The Clue.

You were seen lurking near his competition station right before he collapsed into the flour sack. Worse, a fellow competitor witnessed you hastily throwing a suspicious empty foil packet into the trash can.

4. Costume Ideas.

Intimidating, high-fashion elegance. Think oversized designer sunglasses, an impeccable silk scarf, and tailored attire. Carry a sophisticated Moleskine notebook and an expensive pen to silently judge everyone else's outfits and alibis throughout the evening.

SECRET: PRIVATE LETTER

Your private letter containing your secret motives, objectives, and clues is strictly confidential.

It will be handed to you in a sealed envelope upon your arrival at 113 Shinny Avenue, Stittsville.

Do not discuss your private instructions with anyone until the game officially begins.
